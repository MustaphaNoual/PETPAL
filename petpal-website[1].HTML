Where your pet gets to shine.

PetPal is the social media app built for people who know that a pet is never just a pet. Share your animal's best moments, connect locally, and find everything you need to keep your companion happy.

Give your pet their own profile

Every animal is unique, and PetPal treats them that way. When you join, the first thing you do is create a profile for your pet - not for yourself. You get to fill it with everything that makes your companion who they are: their breed, their favourite foods, the toys they cannot live without, their hidden talents, and the little quirks that only you know about.

PetPal is one of the very few platforms that welcomes all animals, not just dogs and cats. Whether your best friend has four legs, two wings, or no legs at all, there is a place for them here.

Share the moments that matter most

Post photos, share videos, write stories, and record your own voice to narrate moments. There is no clutter, no unrelated content getting in the way - just pets, and the people who love them.

Connect with pet owners near you

Search for pet owners by location, arrange meetups, share tips about the best spots to walk, and make plans to attend local events. Finding your community used to mean hovering around the dog park; PetPal makes it intentional.

Stay informed with pet news & tips

The app's news feed surfaces the latest pet-related trends, health tips, and news stories from around the world. Curated to be relevant, trustworthy, and genuinely useful - not clickbait.

Find local pet services, charities & adoption centres

Beyond the social features, PetPal functions as a practical resource for pet owners who want to take good care of their animals. The app makes it easy to find local groomers, veterinary clinics, pet stores, daycare facilities and dog walkers - all searchable by location.

For those who want to give something back, PetPal also connects users with pet charities looking for volunteers and donations, and with adoption centres where animals are waiting to find their forever homes.

About PetPal – Our Story

PetPal was born from a simple belief: the bond between a person and their animal deserves its own space on the internet. Not a corner of someone else's social network, but a platform built entirely around it.

Where the idea came from

Pet owners have always been a community. Long before social media existed, people gathered at dog parks, at veterinary waiting rooms, and in the pages of breed-specific magazines to share the experience of living with animals. What changed is that the internet brought everyone together - but not always in a space designed for them.

General social networks are extraordinary tools, but they were not built with pet owners in mind. Posts about your cat compete with political opinions, brand advertisements, and news from people you barely know. The joy of sharing a moment with your animal can feel diluted when it lands in that kind of noise. PetPal was created to solve that problem.

The idea was straightforward: build the platform that pet lovers actually deserve. One where the algorithms serve animals, not advertisers. One where every user shows up because they genuinely want to celebrate and connect around the creatures they care about most.

What we believe in

At PetPal, we believe that pets make people better. They teach patience, they demand presence, and they offer a kind of loyalty and unconditional love that is genuinely rare. We take that seriously, and it shapes everything we build.

We believe a good platform should feel warm, not clinical. It should feel like stepping into a conversation among people who already share something in common. That is why PetPal is designed around community rather than content volume.

We also believe in inclusion. Not every pet is a golden retriever, and not every pet owner fits a single profile. PetPal is for dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, fish, horses, and every animal in between. It is for new owners who are overwhelmed, experienced owners wanting to share, and animal lovers who do not yet have a pet but know they belong in this community.

The team behind PetPal

PetPal was built by a small team of animal lovers with a genuine passion for what they were creating. Every decision, from the design of the pet profile to the layout of the news feed, was made with one question in mind: does this make things better for pet owners and their animals?

Our mission going forward

Our mission is to make PetPal the home of the global pet-loving community. A place where someone in one country can celebrate their animal with someone on the other side of the world. We are just getting started. If you love pets, PetPal is your place.

Features

Everything the app offers

PetPal is more than a social app - it is a complete toolkit for the modern pet owner. From personalised profiles to local discovery, every feature is designed to make life better for pets and the people who love them.

Personalised profiles for every pet

The profile is the heart of PetPal. When you sign up, you create a dedicated profile for your pet - not a human account with an animal in the photos, but a genuine page for your companion.

Add their name, species, breed, age, favourite foods and toys, special talents, and anything else that makes them who they are. PetPal supports all types of animals, and you can create multiple profiles if you have more than one pet.

Photo, video and story sharing

PetPal's sharing tools are built for the pace of real pet ownership. You can post photos and videos directly from your phone, write short stories about your animal's latest adventure, and even record your own voice to narrate moments.

Your posts reach a community that is there specifically for this content. Unlike general social networks, PetPal's feed is entirely focused on animals, resulting in a more engaged audience.

A local community built around your neighbourhood

PetPal's community features are designed to turn online connections into real-world relationships. Find pet owners who live near you, arrange playdates, and make plans to attend local events like dog shows and pet conventions.

For pet owners who have recently moved, this feature is particularly valuable. The local directory also surfaces pet-related businesses in your area, giving you recommendations from people in your community.

Even for animal lovers without a pet

Not everyone who loves animals is in a position to own one right now. Life circumstances, living situations and schedules do not always make it possible - but that does not make the love of animals any less real. PetPal gives animal lovers without a pet their own virtual companion to care for within the app. You can still be a full member of the community!
